About Me

Since graduating from Ball State University in 2013 with a Bachelor's Degree in English, I began my professional career working in customer service and sales for companies like The Walt Disney Company and Enterprise Holdings. I’ve spent the last five years of my professional career in Human Resources, where I specialize in Talent Acquisition, helping companies hire the best and brightest individuals to further their organization’s mission and vision.

My strong business acumen and communication skills are enchanced by a solid technical foundation learned in Eleven Fifty Academy's Web Development Program. This intense, 24-week course involved learning JavaScript and HTML/CSS in the evenings, allowing me to remain employed full-time until I was ready to transition fully. I am excited to enter into this next phase of my career and look forward to the opportunities ahead!

Additionally, I bring a positive attitude and unparalleled work ethic. I am an intrinsically motivated, tenacious problem solver who enjoys working independently and as part of a team. Most recently, I have been working as an Implementation Project Manager for Jobvite, a Recruitment Software SaaS Company.
